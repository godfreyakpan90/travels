How can a foreign worker qualify for Work in UK Visa?

To qualify for work visa in UK, apply for a skilled worker visa. A skilled worker visa is a visa that allows foreign individuals to come or stay in the UK for work with an approved or legal employer. To qualify for this visa, you must get a job offer from a legal employer in the UK, have a certificate of sponsorship from the employer stating the role of the job in the UK, and have a salary scale on the job offer letter. Also, proof of a language test is required to apply for a skilled worker visa.

Other Methods to Apply for Work Visa in the UK

You can apply for a work visa in UK through the Youth Mobility Scheme. This only applies to foreigners from Australia, Canada, Hong Kong, Japan, Monaco, New Zealand, the Republic of Korea, San Marino, and Taiwan. This type of visa is only given to those who want to live and work in the UK for 2 years, are 18–30 years old, have £2,530 in savings, and meet other eligibility criteria.
